About TechNinzaz

On 12th June 2015 a group of four passionate individuals started of with an idea "TechNinzaz" wherein there focus was to ease out the switch between a 'College Graduate' to a 'Technical Expert'. In the past year we have build a team of Industry Experts who share the same passion of engaging their career expertise into skill building for students. The highlights of this venture are

  • Incorporating industry level standards into coaching..
  • Delivering a customized module to the individual students based upon their requirement.
  • Providing Hands on Technical Support in each step of the process.
  • Grooming students to make them "Market Ready".
  • Guiding students with up-to-date knowledge of the current market requirements.

We at Techninzaz believe in helping each student achieve the desired results and excel in their respective domain. Specializing in PUC, B.C.A, BSc, M.C.A, MSc, BTech(Computer Science) subjects along with subject knowledge relevant for industry usage, we will ensure you get a holistic coaching and mentoring for your career ahead.

What makes us different :

  • Young and Qualified Coaches for each subject who will mentor you throughout..
  • Practise questionnaire of previous years of each course .
  • Small batches give each student a more interactive classes.
  • Special guest lectures by Top College Professors every month .
  • 24*7 doubt solving with access to Study groups only for Techninzaz Students
